Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my MT5 account?

Navigate to `Settings > MetaTrader5 Integration` and enter your broker details, MT5 login ID, and password. Your data will then be automatically synchronized.

When are subscription fees deducted?

Subscription fees are deducted once your cumulative realized profit exceeds your tier's threshold (e.g., ₹10,000 for Performance Tier 1). The deduction happens automatically from your linked payment method.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can manage your subscription plan from the `Billing & Subscriptions` section. Options for upgrading or downgrading will be available there.
